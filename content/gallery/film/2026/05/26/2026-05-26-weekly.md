Shot with Pentax LX + CVS 400 film
<!--more-->

While testing Zeiss Ultron 50mm F1.8 against Steinheil Quinon 55mm F1.9 on my last 2 rolls of CVS 400, I made a serious mistake to shoot the same roll twice with both lenses, ending up with a full roll of double-exposed film and another roll of blank film. What a bitter lesson!

Maybe I should make the good habit to rewind the film tip fully back into the cassette in the future to avoid such dumb issue happening again.

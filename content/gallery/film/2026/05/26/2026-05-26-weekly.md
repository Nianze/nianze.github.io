Shot with Pentax LX + CVS 400 film
<!--more-->

I was trying to test the Zeiss Ultron 50mm F1.8 against Steinheil Quinon 55mm F1.9 on my last stock of CVS 400, and made a serious mistake to shoot the same roll twice with two lenses, end up with a full roll of double exposed film with another roll of blake film. What a bitter lesson.

Maybe I should make the good habit to roll the film tip fully back into the cassette in the future to avoid such dumb issue happening again.
